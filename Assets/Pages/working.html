How Does it Work?


RFID Tag detection:

When an authorized user approaches the door, they simply need to tap their RFID tag to the RFID reader installed on or near the door frame. The RFID reader then sends a signal to the system's microcontroller, which processes the information.

Authentication Process:

When an authorized user approaches the door, they simply need to tap their RFID tag to the RFID reader installed on or near the door frame. The RFID reader then sends a signal to the system's microcontroller, which processes the information.

Solenoid Lock Activation:

Upon successful authentication, the microcontroller triggers the solenoid lock mechanism, which releases the bolt and allows the door to be opened. The solenoid lock operates on the principle of electromagnetism, where an electrical current is applied to the solenoid, causing it to retract and unlock the door.
